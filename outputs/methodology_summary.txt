Fraud Monitoring Methodology Summary
===================================

Why this version is stronger
---------------------------
Feature cutoffs are fit on the training split only, the operational threshold is chosen on the validation split, and final performance is reported on a locked test split. That separation avoids the most common credibility problem in student fraud projects: choosing settings on the same data used for final reporting.
